FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 01
    How much does a tattoo cost? Pricing · quotes · deposits
    +

    The price of tattoos varies as I price by piece, not by hour. Message me via WhatsApp and I'll be able to give you a quote.

    Deposits are a must and are non-refundable.

  • 02
    Do you do walk-ins? Appointments · walk-ins · availability
    +

    I tend not to do walk-ins as I am usually quite busy. I don't want to waste your time so please book in.

  • 03
    How do I prepare for my appointment? Preparation · health · what to bring
    +
    • — Eat a proper meal beforehand and stay well hydrated.
    • — Wear clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed.
    • — Avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours before your session.
    • — Do not book if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.
    • — If you are on blood thinners or have a skin condition affecting the area, let me know in advance and I'll advise.
    • — Worth bringing: earphones, iPads, audiobooks, or anything that helps you pass the time.
    • — Feel free to let me know if you want a silent appointment.
  • 04
    Does it hurt? Pain · tolerance · placement
    +

    Yes it hurts, however it is a manageable pain and it is different for everyone. I would say it's uncomfortable rather than unbearable, and you adjust to it quickly.

    To help with this we can take breaks when and as you need, we go at your pace.

    If you are worried about a longer session, let me know and we can split them up into sessions you feel more comfortable with.

  • 05
    What should I know before my first tattoo? First time · expectations · nerves
    +

    It is normal to be nervous, regardless of if it's your first tattoo or fifth. I will walk you through every step, you can take your time choosing placement, design, colours, anything you need. I like to say getting a tattoo is teamwork, we work together to make the best tattoo for you.

  • 06
    What's the minimum age? Age · ID · legal
    +

    18 years old. No exceptions regardless of parental consent. Valid photo ID is required on the day. If you can't prove your age, the session cannot go ahead. Deposit is lost.

Aftercare

Keep it clean.
Let it heal.

A new tattoo is an open wound. How you treat it in the first few days determines how it looks for the rest of your life. Get it right and it'll stay sharp for decades.

First 24 hours
Immediate care
  • Leave the wrap on for as long as specified
  • When removing, wash gently with unscented soap and warm water
  • Pat dry with a clean paper towel. Don't rub.
  • Apply a thin layer of Palmer's Cocoa Butter. Highly recommended.
24 – 72 hours
The healing window
  • Moisturise 2–3× daily with Palmer's Cocoa Butter
  • Don't scratch, pick, or peel. Let it flake naturally.
  • Keep it out of direct sunlight while healing
  • No baths, pools, or sea water for at least 3 weeks
Long term
Keeping it sharp
  • SPF 30+ on healed tattoos whenever you're in the sun
  • Keep skin moisturised. Dry skin dulls the contrast.
  • Sunbeds and UV degrade ink faster than anything else
  • First touch-up is free. Get in touch when fully healed.

If you're ever unsure about how your tattoo is healing, message me directly. Don't wait, it's always better to ask early. Reach me on WhatsApp.
