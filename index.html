Specialties

Areas of Expertise

  • ~
    Fine Line
    Single needle · ultra-precise · long-lasting →

    Single-needle work at its most refined. Ultra-thin lines that hold crisp definition over years without spreading or blurring. Ideal for botanical studies, geometric forms, and script — anything that rewards a second look up close.

  • ~
    Fine Line Art
    Illustrative · considered · one of one →

    Illustrative line work where drawing sensibility meets the permanence of skin. Botanical, abstract, and figurative pieces designed from scratch — suited to your concept, placement, and the way the design will move with the body.

  • ~
    Micro Realism
    Miniature · photographic · exact →

    Photographic precision compressed into small scale. Portraits, animals, and objects rendered with genuine depth and tonal range — detail that reads clearly at a glance and holds up under close inspection, even years later.

  • ~
    Realism
    Lifelike · detailed · striking →

    A lifelike rendering of anything you can photograph. Depth, tonal range, and texture reproduced with accuracy — from portraits to natural subjects to still life. The goal is always skin that looks like it remembers the subject.

  • ~
    Portrait Work
    Likeness · depth · held in the skin →

    Capturing a face and keeping it. Portraits require exact technique and a precise understanding of how skin holds tone over time. Done properly, they don't just look like someone — they feel like them.

  • ~
    Custom Design
    Collaborative · your concept · built from scratch →

    Your idea built from nothing. Bring a reference image, a mood board, a feeling — or just words. The design is collaborative and made entirely for you: nothing pre-drawn, nothing recycled. Every piece starts as a blank page.
