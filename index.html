About

Emmy
Fine line & realism.

Based at Sainthood Tattoo on Princes Avenue, Hull. Specialising in fine line, micro realism, realism, and portrait work — pieces built on precision and designed to age well.

Every tattoo is drawn from scratch. Nothing is pre-drawn or reused. Consultations are free, and the design process is collaborative from the first message.

FAQ
  • 01
    How much does a tattoo cost? Pricing · quotes · deposits
    +

    I price by piece, not by the hour. Every quote is based on size, complexity, and placement. Message me via WhatsApp or Instagram and I'll give you an honest estimate before anything is agreed. Deposits are non-refundable.

  • 02
    Do you do walk-ins? Appointments · walk-ins · availability
    +

    Walk-ins are welcome, but a booking guarantees your tattoo. A walk-in depends on availability on the day. If you're nearby and want to try your luck, feel free to come in. A quick message beforehand avoids a wasted trip.

  • 03
    How do I prepare? Preparation · health · what to bring
    +
    • — Eat a proper meal and stay hydrated
    • — Wear clothing that gives easy access to the area
    • — No alcohol for at least 24 hours before
    • — Do not book if pregnant or breastfeeding
    • — Let me know if you're on blood thinners or have skin conditions
    • — Earphones, a blanket, anything that helps you settle in
  • 04
    Does it hurt? Pain · tolerance · placement
    +

    Honestly, yes — but it's manageable. Most describe it as uncomfortable rather than unbearable, and you adjust quickly. We can take breaks whenever you need them. Longer sessions can be split into two or more shorter appointments.

  • 05
    First tattoo — what to expect? First time · expectations · nerves
    +

    It's normal to be nervous. Everyone is the first time. The process is more considered than most people expect — I'll walk you through every step. Take your time choosing the design and placement.

  • 06
    What's the minimum age? Age · ID · legal
    +

    18, strictly. No exceptions regardless of parental consent. Valid photo ID is required on the day. If you can't prove your age, the session cannot go ahead.

Aftercare

Keep it clean.
Let it heal.

First 24 hours
Immediate care
  • Leave the wrap on for as long as specified
  • Wash gently with unscented soap and warm water
  • Pat dry with a clean paper towel — don't rub
  • Apply a thin layer of Palmer's Cocoa Butter
24 – 72 hours
The healing window
  • Moisturise 2–3× daily with Palmer's Cocoa Butter
  • Don't scratch, pick, or peel — let it flake naturally
  • Keep out of direct sunlight while healing
  • No baths, pools, or sea water for at least 3 weeks
Long term
Keeping it sharp
  • SPF 30+ on healed tattoos in the sun
  • Keep skin moisturised — dry skin dulls contrast
  • Sunbeds and UV degrade ink faster than anything else
  • First touch-up is free — get in touch when fully healed
