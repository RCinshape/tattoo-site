FAQ

Common Questions

  • 01
    How much does a tattoo cost? Pricing · quotes · deposits
    +

    I price by piece, not by the hour. Every quote is based on size, complexity, and placement. Message me via WhatsApp or Instagram and I'll give you an honest estimate before anything is agreed. Deposits are non-refundable.

  • 02
    Do you do walk-ins? Appointments · walk-ins · availability
    +

    Walk-ins are welcome, but a booking guarantees your tattoo. A walk-in depends on availability on the day. If you're nearby and want to try your luck, feel free to come in. A quick message beforehand avoids a wasted trip.

  • 03
    How do I prepare for my appointment? Preparation · health · what to bring
    +
    • — Eat a proper meal beforehand and stay well hydrated
    • — Wear clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed
    • — Avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours before your session
    • — Do not book if you are pregnant or breastfeeding
    • — If you are on blood thinners or have a skin condition affecting the area, let me know in advance and I'll advise
    • — Worth bringing: earphones, an iPad, an audiobook, or anything that helps you pass the time. A blanket is welcome too
  • 04
    Does it hurt? Pain · tolerance · placement
    +

    Honestly, yes, but it's manageable for most people. Most describe it as uncomfortable rather than unbearable, and you adjust to it quickly. We can take breaks whenever you need them. We go at your pace. If you're worried about a longer session, we can split it into two or more shorter appointments instead.

  • 05
    What should I know before my first tattoo? First time · expectations · nerves
    +

    It's normal to be nervous. Everyone is the first time. The process is more considered than most people expect, I'll walk you through every step. Take your time choosing the design and placement.

  • 06
    What's the minimum age? Age · ID · legal
    +

    18, strictly. No exceptions regardless of parental consent. Valid photo ID is required on the day. If you can't prove your age, the session cannot go ahead.

Aftercare

Keep it clean.
Let it heal.

A new tattoo is an open wound. How you treat it in the first few days determines how it looks for the rest of your life. Get it right and it'll stay sharp for decades.

First 24 hours
Immediate care
  • Leave the wrap on for as long as specified
  • When removing, wash gently with unscented soap and warm water
  • Pat dry with a clean paper towel. Don't rub.
  • Apply a thin layer of Palmer's Cocoa Butter. Highly recommended.
24 – 72 hours
The healing window
  • Moisturise 2–3× daily with Palmer's Cocoa Butter
  • Don't scratch, pick, or peel. Let it flake naturally.
  • Keep it out of direct sunlight while healing
  • No baths, pools, or sea water for at least 3 weeks
Long term
Keeping it sharp
  • SPF 30+ on healed tattoos whenever you're in the sun
  • Keep skin moisturised. Dry skin dulls the contrast.
  • Sunbeds and UV degrade ink faster than anything else
  • First touch-up is free. Get in touch when fully healed.

If you're ever unsure about how your tattoo is healing, message me directly. Don't wait, it's always better to ask early. Reach me on WhatsApp or Instagram.
